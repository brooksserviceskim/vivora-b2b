01 — Brand Story

Born from real skin concerns

As skin dryness and sensitivity grew over time, we realized daily skincare is not a cosmetic routine but an essential factor shaping skin condition. Rather than temporary fixes, we searched for ingredients that genuinely support the skin.

Through extensive raw-material research, expert consultation and continuous testing, VIVORA was built on three core principles.

EfficacyCarefully selected, efficacy-driven ingredients
BalanceWell-balanced, skin-friendly formulations
ComfortA comfortable daily user experience

"VIVORA focuses on balanced formulations that support the skin's natural ability to maintain its own healthy condition."

02 — Technology & Core Science

Fermented RG3, by skin science

Our core formulations are powered by patented fermented RG3 — a ginseng-derived active developed on research technology from the Gyeongsangbuk-do Agricultural Research & Extension Services. Enzymatic fermentation enhances the active components to support skin conditioning.
